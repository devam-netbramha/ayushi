HDFC Bank (DDP Smart Saathi)

Platform to connect business correspondents (BCs) and business facilitators (BFs) to the bank’s network.

HDFC Bank has engaged with multiple partners to form a strategic alliance to expand its network.

Team wants to take banking to the rural deep geo- locations wherein a non-banking partner can perform banking functions at ease, and customer is able to experience services seamlessly.

Team Members

04

Time

04 Months

Role

Goals

An enhanced digital ecosystem that provides a satisfying user experience based on the aspects such as:

  • Banking accessibility in the remotest of places
  • Increasing financial literacy
  • Enabling access to easy credit

Mission

  • Collating 40 banking products and services on a single platform
  • Engaging 2 lakh villages over the next 12 to 18 months

Approach

User Roles

Onboarding multiple partners to the platform requires myriad complex processes. To simplify these processes, we tried a multi user-centric approach.
